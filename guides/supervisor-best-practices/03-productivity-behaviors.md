- **Use checklists** - Don't rely on memory for critical steps
- **Learn from mistakes** - When error happens, figure out why (not just who)

- **Start with priorities** - Do critical orders when team is fresh (first 2-3 hours)

- **Encourage breaks** - Well-rested team is more productive
