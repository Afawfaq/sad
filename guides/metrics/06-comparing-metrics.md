## ðŸ“† This Month vs. Last Month

**Good comparison because:**
- Same facility, same team
- Shows trends over time
- Easy to spot improvements or declines

**What to look for:**
- 5-10% improvement = Great trend
- Flat = Stable, but look for opportunities
- 5-10% decline = Investigate why
- 20%+ decline = Major problem
