### The Trade-off: Speed vs. Accuracy

**Common mistake:** Push only for speed
**Result:** Errors go up, customer complaints increase

**The balance:**
- Set accuracy as non-negotiable minimum (95%+)
- Then push speed within that constraint
- Never sacrifice quality for quantity

---

### The Trade-off: Current Orders vs. Future Prep

**Common mistake:** Only focus on today's shipments
**Result:** Tomorrow starts behind, always playing catch-up

**The balance:**
- Priority 1: Today's deadlines
- Priority 2: Tomorrow's prep (if time)
- Don't sacrifice today, but leave team in good spot for tomorrow
