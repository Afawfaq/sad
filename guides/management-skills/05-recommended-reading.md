## ðŸŽ¯ Skill Development Plan

### Month 1-2: Foundation
- Master your templates
- Learn your WMS/system inside out
- Build relationships with team
- Observe other good managers
- Get feedback from your manager

### Month 3-4: Communication
- Practice SBAR reporting
- Run daily huddles confidently
- Have first difficult conversation
- Start reading management book
- Join industry group/forum

### Month 5-6: Leadership
- Mentor a new team member
- Implement one process improvement
- Track and improve your KPIs
- Take online course
- Teach something to team

### Ongoing
- Read 1 management book per quarter
- Attend 1 training/webinar per month
- Get feedback from team regularly
- Set personal development goals
- Document what you learn
