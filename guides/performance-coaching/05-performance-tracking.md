How to track and analyze employee performance.

**What to track:**
- Individual pick/pack rates
- Accuracy (errors per person)
- Attendance/punctuality
- Attitude/behavior notes

**How:**
- Keep simple notes
- Don't need fancy system
- Just write: "Date - [Name] - [issue or win]"

**Example Notes:**
- "11/23 - John - Pick rate 65/hr, great job"
- "11/23 - Sarah - Late again (15 min), talked to her"
- "11/23 - Mike - Wrong item picked (3rd time this week)"

- Praise improvers publicly
- Coach underperformers privately
