# Financial & Security Edge Cases

## üí∞ Suspected Theft by Employee
**DO NOT confront directly**

**Action:**
1. Document what you observed (date, time, what you saw)
2. Review security footage if available
3. Report to HR and upper management immediately
4. Let them investigate - this is above your pay grade
5. Do NOT discuss with other employees
6. Continue normal supervision until directed otherwise

**Legal note:** False accusations can result in lawsuits

---

## üö® Suspected Theft by Customer
**Action:**
1. Do NOT physically stop or detain
2. Note description, time, items
3. Review security footage
4. Contact security/police per company policy
5. Document incident
6. Let authorities handle it

**Never:** Chase, physically confront, or accuse without proof

---

## üíµ Finding Large Amount of Cash/Valuable Item
**Action:**
1. Don't touch it (fingerprints)
2. Call manager and security immediately
3. Secure area
4. Document where found, when, by whom
5. Have witness present
6. Turn over to management with witness signature

**Protect yourself:** Never handle alone

---

## üç∫ Employee Shows Up Intoxicated/High
**Action:**
1. Pull them aside privately
2. "I'm concerned about your safety. You don't seem well. Let me call someone to pick you up."
3. Do NOT let them work
4. Do NOT let them drive
5. Call manager/HR immediately
6. Arrange safe transportation
7. Document incident
8. Follow company policy on discipline

**Safety first:** Impaired employees are dangerous to themselves and others
