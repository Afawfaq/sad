Pickup Procedures:

When Order is Ready:
1. Move order to designated pickup area
2. Label clearly with order # and customer name
3. Update system status to "Ready for Pickup"
4. Notify customer (text/email/call)
5. Log on this sheet

When Customer Arrives:
1. Ask for order number or name
2. Verify customer ID matches name on order
3. Have customer sign for order
4. Check items with customer (optional but recommended)
5. Hand off order
6. Update system status to "Picked Up"
7. Log completion

If Customer is Late/No Show:
- Same Day: Hold in pickup area, attempt to contact
- Next Day: Move to holding area, send reminder
- After 3 Days: Contact about alternative (ship or extended hold)
- After 7 Days: Process as abandoned/return to stock (per policy)
