## Quality Standards

### Picking Accuracy
- ✅ Correct item (SKU matches order)
- ✅ Correct quantity
- ✅ No damaged items selected
- ✅ Check expiration dates (if applicable)
- ✅ Items from correct location

### Packing Quality
- ✅ Appropriate box size (not too big/small)
- ✅ Adequate protective material (bubble wrap, paper, etc.)
- ✅ Items secured (won't shift in transit)
- ✅ Fragile items properly protected
- ✅ Heavy items on bottom
- ✅ Box properly sealed with tape
- ✅ Label affixed correctly
- ✅ No damage to items during packing

### Special Handling Items
- ✅ Fragile stickers applied when needed
- ✅ "This Side Up" indicators (if needed)
- ✅ Temperature-sensitive items handled properly
- ✅ High-value items double-checked
- ✅ Multi-piece sets complete
