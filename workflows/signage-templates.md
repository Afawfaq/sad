### DIY Sign-Making Tools

**Free Design Software:**
- Canva (templates available)

**Printing Options:**
- Office printer (8.5" Ã— 11" or 11" Ã— 17")
- FedEx/UPS Store (large format printing)
- Online services (Vistaprint, BuildASign)
- Local sign shop (professional signs)

**Materials:**
- **Indoor:** Foam board, laminated paper, adhesive vinyl
- **Outdoor/Harsh:** Aluminum, rigid plastic (Coroplast), PVC
- **Temporary:** Paper, cardboard (short-term use)
- **Floor:** Industrial tape, epoxy paint

### Sign Design Best Practices

**Readability:**
- Font size minimum 1" per 10 feet viewing distance
- Sans-serif fonts (Arial, Helvetica) for clarity
- High contrast (black on white, white on dark colors)
- Avoid all caps for body text (harder to read)

**Layout:**
- Simple, uncluttered design
- One main message per sign
- Use icons/symbols when possible (universal)
- White space important (don't cram)

**Durability:**
- Laminate paper signs (protection from dirt, moisture)
- UV-resistant materials for outdoor/window areas
- Rounded corners prevent peeling
- Secure mounting (not just tape)
